About

Research and engineering at the intersection of databases, storage, and distributed systems.

I am a senior software development manager at Oracle, working on the design and implementation of next-generation Oracle database products. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from The Ohio State University and an undergraduate degree in Computer Science from BITS-Pilani (undergraduate thesis completed at the University of Westminster in London). My research interests are related to storage systems, particularly in novel designs for new technologies (PMEM, NVMe, and RDMA) and problems of scale in distributed systems. I have published several papers in top conferences and journals including SIGMOD, VLDB, HPDC, SC, IPDPS, BigData, and Journal of Grid Computing.
